## Client:
The client is Hoang Minh Company, a company specializing in implementing e-government projects in Vietnam. The company has nearly 100 employees working in 5 departments: Administration – Human Resources, Information Technology, Content Review, Finance – Accounting, and Project Implementation. Contact person: Bui Ngoc Le.

## Situation:
### The situation the client presented to us is:
- When implementing projects for partners, the company will establish a project team for implementation. The project includes: a Project Manager, and 2 implementation teams: a digitization team and a cataloging team, each with a team leader. To enable construction, the company will hand over tools and equipment to the teams to carry out the work, including: personal computers, printers, network equipment, servers, scanners, office supplies, etc.
- After the project is completed, all equipment must be handed over to the Human Resources department for inspection and assessment of its condition. If there is any damage, it must be repaired and upgraded promptly. For lost equipment, the employee using it is responsible for compensation according to regulations.

## Requirements Description:
According to the client's requirements, the Asset Management System will need to have:
### Functional Requirements:
The asset information management function includes: Equipment code; Equipment name; Description; Asset value; Purchase date; Depreciation period (according to accounting regulations); Sub-components of the equipment; Sub-components of the equipment that can be replaced are also considered as separate asset equipment and have their own equipment code.

- Asset and equipment handover management function: handing over assets and equipment to project teams for project execution.

- Return and delivery management function: handing over and returning equipment at project completion, assessing equipment condition upon return.

- Statistical reporting function listing assets and equipment for a project.

- Statistical reporting function listing assets and equipment by condition.

- Statistical reporting function listing assets and equipment that have reached the end of their depreciation period.

- Search function for equipment information and status.

## Regarding non-functional requirements:

- A solution for quickly inventorying and recording asset and equipment handover.

- Flexibility in system access.

- System development and deployment time should not exceed 3 months.
