# Project Overview
---

## 1. Client Overview

**Client Name:** Hoang Minh Company  
**Business Domain:** E-Government Project Implementation  
**Location:** Vietnam  
**Company Size:** Approximately 100 employees  

**Departments:**
- Administration & Human Resources
- Information Technology
- Content Review
- Finance & Accounting
- Project Implementation

**Client Representative:** Mr. Bui Ngoc Le

Hoang Minh Company specializes in implementing e-government projects for public sector partners. The company frequently forms project-based teams to execute large-scale digitization and system implementation initiatives.

---

## 2. Business Situation

When executing projects for partners, Hoang Minh Company establishes a dedicated project team consisting of:
- A Project Manager
- A Digitization Team (with Team Leader)
- A Cataloging Team (with Team Leader)

To support project execution, the company hands over tools and equipment to project teams, including:
- Personal computers and laptops
- Printers and scanners
- Network devices
- Servers
- Office equipment and supplies

### Current Challenges
- Asset handover and tracking are handled manually.
- Equipment condition tracking after project completion is inconsistent.
- Difficulty in identifying asset responsibility when damage or loss occurs.
- Limited visibility into asset usage, condition, and depreciation status.

After project completion, all assets must be returned to the Administration & Human Resources Department for inspection.  
- Damaged equipment must be repaired or upgraded.
- Lost equipment requires compensation from the responsible employee according to company regulations.

---

## 3. Project Objective

The objective of this project is to develop an **Asset & Equipment Management System** that:
- Digitizes asset lifecycle management
- Improves control over asset handover and return processes
- Enhances transparency and accountability
- Supports reporting and decision-making for management

---

## 4. System Scope Overview

### 4.1 Functional Requirements

The system shall support the following core functions:

#### Asset Information Management
- Manage asset attributes including:
  - Equipment code
  - Equipment name
  - Description
  - Asset value
  - Purchase date
  - Depreciation period (in accordance with accounting regulations)
  - Sub-components of equipment
- Replaceable sub-components are treated as independent assets with their own equipment codes.

#### Asset & Equipment Handover Management
- Manage the handover of assets and equipment to project teams.
- Track asset responsibility by employee and project.

#### Asset Return & Delivery Management
- Register asset returns upon project completion.
- Assess equipment condition during return.
- Update asset status after inspection.

#### Reporting & Statistics
- Generate asset and equipment reports by project.
- Generate asset and equipment reports by condition.
- Generate reports for assets that have reached the end of their depreciation period.

#### Search Function
- Search assets by equipment code, name, status, condition, or project.

---

## 5. Non-Functional Requirements

The system shall meet the following non-functional requirements:

- **Efficiency:** Support fast asset inventory and handover recording.
- **Accessibility:** Flexible system access for different user roles.
- **Performance:** Responsive system for daily operational usage.
- **Delivery Timeline:** System development and deployment must be completed within **3 months**.

- Faster asset handover and return processes
- Accurate reporting for management and accounting
- Strong foundation for future system scalability
